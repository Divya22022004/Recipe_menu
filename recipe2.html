Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biriyani masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and add whole spices.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  6. Add chicken pieces and biriyani masala. Cook until the chicken is done.
  7. Add yogurt and mix well.
  8. Drain the soaked rice and add to the pot.
  9. Add 4 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  10. Reduce heat, cover, and cook until the rice is fully cooked and water is absorbed.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves before serving.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 350 per serving

Protein: 20g

Carbohydrates: 45g

Fat: 10g
