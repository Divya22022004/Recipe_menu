Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 12 large mushrooms
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Clean mushrooms and remove stems. Chop stems finely.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and chopped mushroom stems until tender.
  4. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, sautéed garlic and stems, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stuff the mushroom caps with the mixture and place on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until mushrooms are tender and tops are golden brown.
  7. Serve warm.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 70 per serving

Protein: 3g

Carbohydrates: 3g

Fat: 5g
