Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Boil milk in a pan. Add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle the milk.
  2. Strain the curdled milk through a cheesecloth and rinse with cold water.
  3. Squeeze out excess water and knead the chenna (cottage cheese) until smooth.
  4. Shape the chenna into small balls.
  5. In a pan, combine sugar and water to make the syrup. Boil until sugar dissolves.
  6. Add the balls to the simmering syrup and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Let the rasgullas cool in the syrup before serving.
