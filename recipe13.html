Kheer

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup basmati rice
  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped almonds
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, bring milk to a boil. Add rice and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. When the rice is cooked and the milk has reduced, add sugar and cardamom powder.
  4. Add chopped almonds, raisins, and saffron if using. Cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool. Serve warm or chilled.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 200 per serving

Protein: 5g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Fat: 8g
