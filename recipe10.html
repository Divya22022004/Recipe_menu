Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, sriracha, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add chicken wings and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Place wings on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, turning halfway through, until crispy and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 250 per serving

Protein: 20g

Carbohydrates: 12g

Fat: 15g
