Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, beans)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add boiled noodles and toss to combine with vegetables and sauce.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes and serve hot.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 300 per serving

Protein: 8g

Carbohydrates: 50g

Fat: 8g
