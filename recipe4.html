Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice and fenugreek seeds together for 4-6 hours.
  2. Wash and soak urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
  3. Grind urad dal to a smooth batter and transfer to a large bowl.
  4. Grind rice and fenugreek seeds to a coarse batter and mix with urad dal batter.
  5. Add salt and mix well. Let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  6. Grease idly molds and pour the batter into the molds.
  7. Steam for 10-12 minutes or until idlis are cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
