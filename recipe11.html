Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, flour, and baking powder.
  2. Add ghee and mix until crumbly. Gradually add milk to form a soft dough.
  3. Shape the dough into small balls, making sure they are smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  5. In a separate pan, combine sugar, water, and cardamom powder to make the syrup. Boil until slightly thickened.
  6. Soak the fried balls in the warm syrup for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 180 per piece
