Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of oil and mix well.
  3. Add water little by little and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  6. Roll out each ball into a thin circle.
  7. Heat a pan and cook the parota with a little oil until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Serve hot with curry or chutney.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 200 per serving

Protein: 5g

Carbohydrates: 30g

Fat: 7g
