Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Italian bread
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice the Italian bread into 1/2-inch thick slices and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Toast the bread slices in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
  4. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, and olive oil. Mix well.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste. If desired, stir in balsamic vinegar for extra flavor.
  6. Top each toasted bread slice with the tomato mixture.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra basil if desired.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 120 per serving

Protein: 3g

Carbohydrates: 15g

Fat: 6g
