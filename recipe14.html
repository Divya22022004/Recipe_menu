Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, cornstarch, and baking powder. Add yogurt and water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Let the batter ferment for 2 hours.
  3. In a pan, combine sugar, water, and cardamom powder to make the syrup. Boil until it reaches a one-thread consistency.
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Pour batter into a piping bag and pipe spirals into hot oil. Fry until golden brown.
  5. Soak the fried jalebis in warm syrup for a few seconds.
  6. Serve warm.

Nutritional Information
