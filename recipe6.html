Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan and saut√© cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and bean sprouts for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce and salt, and mix well. Remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Place a small amount of the vegetable mixture in each spring roll wrapper and roll tightly.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the spring rolls until golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with dipping sauce.

Nutritional Information

Calories: 150 per serving

Protein: 2g

Carbohydrates: 20g

Fat: 7g
